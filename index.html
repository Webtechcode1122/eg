About Us

Welcome to Celestial Voyage, the brainchild of Arnav Jain and Arham Jain from Delhi Public School Bhopal Neelbad , crafted exclusively for the Extra Quadrata Tech Fest 2.0 competition. Our infotainment website orbits the exciting realms of the future of space exploration and life on the moon .

As participants in the tech fest, we've navigated the cosmic sea to bring you an immersive experience. Celestial Voyage isn't just a website; it's our entry into the competition, a testament to our passion for the mysteries of space. Explore the latest in space tech, breakthroughs, and the possibilities of lunar life as we compete to inspire and captivate.
